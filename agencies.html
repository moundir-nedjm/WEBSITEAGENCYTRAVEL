Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about partnering with MIRA BOOKING

What are the requirements to register as a travel agency partner?

To register as a MIRA BOOKING partner, you need to be a legitimate travel agency with proper business registration and licensing. During the registration process, you'll need to provide your business registration documents, travel association memberships (if applicable), and company details. Our verification team will review your application within 24-48 hours.

Are there any registration or subscription fees?

MIRA BOOKING operates on a commission-based model, which means there are no upfront registration fees or monthly subscription costs. We earn our revenue through a percentage of each successful booking made through our platform. This commission is already factored into the rates you see, ensuring transparency in pricing.

How competitive are your rates compared to other B2B platforms?

MIRA BOOKING offers some of the most competitive rates in the industry, thanks to our direct contracts with over 30,000 properties worldwide and partnerships with major hotel chains. Our rates are typically 15-25% lower than public OTA prices, giving you a significant margin to work with. We also negotiate special deals and promotions regularly to further enhance your competitive edge.

What commission rates can I expect?

Our standard commission rates range from 10% to 25%, depending on the property, location, and season. High-volume partners can qualify for our tiered commission structure, which provides enhanced rates based on booking volume. Additionally, we run special promotion periods where commissions on selected properties are boosted, sometimes up to 30%.

How does the payment process work?

MIRA BOOKING offers flexible payment options. You can choose between prepaid bookings (where you pay at the time of booking) or postpaid bookings (where payment is collected after the guest's stay). Commission payments are processed on a monthly basis, with detailed statements provided through your dashboard. We support multiple payment methods including bank transfers, credit cards, and popular payment platforms.

Do you provide marketing materials for agencies?

Yes, we provide a comprehensive set of marketing materials to help you promote our offerings to your clients. This includes high-quality property images, destination guides, seasonal promotion materials, and customizable email templates. All these resources are available in our Partner Resource Center, accessible from your dashboard after registration.

How comprehensive is your inventory?

MIRA BOOKING offers access to over 1.2 million accommodations worldwide, spanning across 190+ countries. Our inventory includes hotels, resorts, apartments, villas, and unique properties. We have direct contracts with major hotel chains and independent properties alike, ensuring a diverse range of options for every client need and budget.
