Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register my agency with MIRA BOOKING?

Registration is simple. Click on the "Register" button in the top navigation, complete the registration form with your agency details, and submit required documentation. Our partnership team will review your application and get back to you within 2 business days.

What commission rates does MIRA BOOKING offer?

Our commission structure is competitive and tiered based on booking volume. Standard rates start at 10% and can increase up to 18% for high-volume partners. For detailed information, please contact our partnership team.

How quickly can I get support if I have an issue with a booking?

Our support team is available 24/7 for urgent booking issues. You can reach us through your agency portal, email, phone, or live chat. For critical matters, we guarantee a response time of under 30 minutes.

Does MIRA BOOKING offer API integration for our booking system?

Yes, we offer comprehensive API solutions for seamless integration with your existing booking systems. Our API documentation is available in the agency portal, and our technical team can assist with implementation.

What training resources are available for our agents?

We provide extensive training resources including video tutorials, webinars, documentation, and one-on-one training sessions. All agency partners receive complimentary onboarding training, and additional sessions can be scheduled as needed.
