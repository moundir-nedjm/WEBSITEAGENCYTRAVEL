Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the registration process take?

The registration and verification process typically takes 1-2 business days. For agencies that provide all required documentation promptly, we can often complete the process within 24 hours.

Is there a fee to register with MIRA BOOKING?

No, registration with MIRA BOOKING is completely free. We operate on a commission-based model, so you only pay when you make bookings through our platform.

What documentation is required for verification?

We require proof of your agency's legal business registration, such as a business license or registration certificate. Additionally, we may request proof of any relevant travel industry affiliations (IATA, ASTA, ABTA, etc.) if applicable.

Can I have multiple users for my agency account?

Yes, you can add multiple users to your agency account with different permission levels. This allows you to manage access for different team members based on their roles.
