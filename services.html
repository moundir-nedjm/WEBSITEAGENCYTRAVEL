Comprehensive B2B Travel Solutions

MIRA BOOKING provides a complete suite of travel services designed specifically for travel agencies, tour operators, and corporate travel departments. Our technology-driven platform gives you the tools and inventory you need to deliver exceptional travel experiences to your clients.

Our Core Services

Hotel Reservations

Access to over 500,000 properties worldwide with competitive rates and availability. From luxury resorts to budget-friendly accommodations, our inventory covers all categories.

  • Real-time availability and instant confirmations
  • Special negotiated rates and exclusive deals
  • Detailed property information and high-quality images
  • Flexible cancellation options

Flight Bookings

Comprehensive flight search and booking capabilities with access to multiple global distribution systems (GDS) for the best possible routes and fares.

  • Access to over 500 airlines worldwide
  • Advanced search with flexible date options
  • Direct connections with major carriers
  • Special fares for groups and corporate clients

Car Rentals

Partnerships with major car rental companies worldwide to offer competitive rates and a wide selection of vehicles for your clients.

  • Global coverage in over 180 countries
  • All vehicle types from economy to luxury
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • 24/7 customer support for rental issues

Package Tours

Pre-built and customizable travel packages that combine flights, accommodations, transfers, and activities for a complete travel experience.

  • Ready-made packages to popular destinations
  • Ability to customize every aspect of the itinerary
  • Special packages for honeymoons and group travel
  • Competitive pricing through bulk purchasing

Activities & Excursions

Extensive inventory of tours, activities, and attractions in destinations worldwide to enhance your clients' travel experience.

  • Over 50,000 bookable activities worldwide
  • Skip-the-line tickets for popular attractions
  • Private and group tour options
  • Easy booking and digital vouchers

Transfers & Transportation

Reliable airport and intercity transfer services to ensure seamless travel experiences for your clients from arrival to departure.

  • Private and shared transfer options
  • Punctual and professional service
  • Vehicle options for all group sizes
  • Real-time tracking and flight monitoring

Pricing & Commission Structure

At MIRA BOOKING, we believe in transparent pricing and a fair commission structure that rewards your business growth. Our platform is free to join, with no setup fees or monthly charges.

How We Work

  • No registration or monthly subscription fees
  • Earn competitive commissions on every booking
  • Tiered commission structure based on booking volume
  • Option to set your own markup on published rates
  • Monthly commission payments with detailed statements
  • Volume-based incentives and bonus programs
